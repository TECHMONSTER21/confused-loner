Freedom

Freedom seems meagre
Slavery feels so great
For what i always fought for was freedom
Slave to the very idea of freedom
Free to be a slave
What price does a petal pay to be free
What price does a cadet pay
To be free from the shackles of maternal bond
For everything comes with a price
Price which has to be paid to almighty
Mystery is to me this conundrum
What price does i have to pay?
Inorder to become that Price.
