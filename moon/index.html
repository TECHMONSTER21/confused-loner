Moon

Slowly subdues the pater of grim reaper his duty
Quickly rises the selene in sky as if she is hell's bounty
Little she looks ill at ease
Exquisite her scars look just as if they live with her at peace
Limelight are that glinting mob of stars
Unvieling the arena of immaculate darkness at which she chuckles
Envious must be the cupid of her darts of soft ambrosia rays
Swarmed her radiance the entire stage like a wildfire
Dawn of this bitter love is all her blunder
A real ambrosia of hope for a blue bard
There lies a dire facet of everything there ever will be
Fireflies toiling to transcend the celestial bodies
Clouds passes by as if to protect her from mortals
Cried loudly the curtain closers in fear of losing her
But was there a one who loved her the same in all her phases.
